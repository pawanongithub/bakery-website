Our sweet story

We are Butter Bliss and we love baking cakes, bakes and desserts. With this pure, sweet sentiment, we founded our company and opened our first American-style bakery in Notting Hill,2025. And so arrived the authentic taste of fresh, American home baking in London. We are delighted to help our guests celebrate and spoil themselves everyday! We offer an incredible range of American-style baked treats. Cupcakes, celebration cakes, brownies, pies, cheesecakes… or perhaps the Monthly Special? Honestly, we don’t think you can go wrong. Such is the popularity of exceptional cake baked that very same day, we opened a further five stores across our dear capital: in South Kensington, Spitalfields, Victoria and St Johns Wood and Soho. We can’t wait to welcome you!
